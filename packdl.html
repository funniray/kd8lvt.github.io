THE TOAST PACK


To Import:


1. Download the pack:


Click here to download the Toast Pack!

2. If you down't have it already, download the Curse Voice Launcher:

Download Curse Voice with the Minecraft Plugin here!

3. Install Curse Voice using the exe you got from the above site


4. Log in to curse (make an account if you have to)


5. Click the little creeper face on the far left


6. Click "Create Custom Profile"


7. Click the "Import" link


8. Navigate to the .zip file you downloaded above, and import it


9. Wait for Curse to download the mods...


10. And you are done! From here, whenever you want to play, Click the "Play" button under the profile of "The Real ToastPack" (It may say "ToastPack" instead of "The Real ToastPack", but you get the point.) and the Minecraft launcher will pop up. Click play as if you are playing vanilla, and enjoy!
