Armor Stand Utilities


How To Use: (NOTE: You must be opped and in creative mode to do this)


1. Give yourself a command block using /give @p command_block


2. Place it


3. Copy the command below, using the correct one for your minecraft version (1.9/1.10 vs 1.11) and then paste it into the command block by pressing CTRL+V on Windows/Linux or CMD+V on Mac

4. Press "Needs Redstone" so it changes to "Always Active"


5. Click "Done"


Now that the command has generated, you can click on the two signs to do as they describe. NOTE: The "Destroy" sign destroys the machine. (Duh)
